## 📊 Content Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor
```
📈 Engagement Rate: Target >5% for posts, >10% for stories
💬 Comment Sentiment: Track positive vs negative reactions
🔄 Share Rate: Indicates viral potential
📱 App Download Attribution: Track which content drives installs
🎯 Conversion Rate: From social media to app signup
```

### A/B Testing Ideas
```
🎬 Video Length: 15s vs 30s vs 60s
📝 Caption Length: Short punchy vs detailed storytelling
🎵 Audio Choice: Trending vs emotional vs upbeat
⏰ Posting Time: Peak hours vs off-peak engagement
🎨 Visual Style: Raw/authentic vs polished/branded
```
