## 🎬 Influencer Collaboration Content Guidelines

### Content Package Options

#### Package A: Basic Review (Micro-Influencers)
**Deliverables:**
- 1 TikTok video (15-30 seconds)
- 3 Instagram Stories
- 1 Instagram Post or Reel

**Key Messages:**
- Authentic first-time experience
- Genuine reaction to AI analysis
- Personal insight about their pet
- Honest pros/cons feedback

#### Package B: Challenge Participation (Mid-Tier)
**Deliverables:**
- 1 TikTok challenge video
- 1 Instagram Reel
- 5 Instagram Stories (journey format)
- 1 Feed post with carousel

**Content Themes:**
- "Rating My Pet's AI Analysis"
- "30-Day Pet Care Challenge"
- "AI vs My Knowledge of My Pet"

#### Package C: Campaign Ambassador (Macro)
**Deliverables:**
- 2 TikTok videos
- 2 Instagram Reels
- 1 IGTV or YouTube video
- 10 Instagram Stories
- 2 Feed posts

**Content Themes:**
- Behind-the-scenes app testing
- Educational content about pet behavior
- Community challenges and engagement
- Long-form testimonial content

### Content Requirements & Guidelines

#### Must-Include Elements:
```
✅ Genuine first reaction to AI analysis
✅ Clear app demonstration (screen recording)
✅ Before/after behavior observations
✅ Honest opinion and recommendations
✅ Clear call-to-action with tracking link
✅ FTC disclosure compliance (#ad #sponsored)
```

#### Avoid/Restrictions:
```
❌ Medical claims or veterinary advice
❌ Guarantees about pet health outcomes
❌ Comparison to actual veterinary care
❌ Overly scripted or promotional language
❌ False or exaggerated reactions
```

## 📅 Influencer Campaign Calendar

### Phase 1: Foundation Building (Month 1)
**Week 1-2: Micro-Influencer Seeding**
- Reach out to 50 micro-influencers
- Send free app access and basic brief
- Collect authentic reviews and feedback

**Week 3-4: Content Optimization**
- Analyze performance of initial content
- Identify top-performing formats and messages
- Refine guidelines for larger campaigns

### Phase 2: Momentum Building (Month 2)
**Week 1-2: Mid-Tier Activation**
- Launch paid partnerships with 15-20 mid-tier influencers
- Coordinate content releases for maximum impact
- Begin community challenge campaigns

**Week 3-4: Viral Push**
- Amplify best-performing content with paid promotion
- Encourage user-generated content responses
- Cross-platform content syndication

### Phase 3: Scale & Celebrity (Month 3)
**Week 1-2: Macro-Influencer Launch**
- Execute celebrity pet partnerships
- Coordinate major campaign launch
- Media and PR activation

**Week 3-4: Sustain & Scale**
- Continue successful micro-influencer programs
- Launch referral and affiliate programs
- Plan next quarter campaigns
